How to Play

Welcome to Higher or Lower!

  • Start with 100 points
  • Place your bet (minimum 25 points)
  • Guess if the dealer's card will be higher or lower than your card
  • If you're right, you win your bet
  • If you're wrong, you lose your bet
  • If it's a draw, no points are lost or gained
  • Game ends when you run out of points
